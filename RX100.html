The Yamaha RX 100 was a two-stroke motorcycle made by Yamaha from 1985 to 1996 with technical collaboration and distributed in India by the Escorts Group. At the initial stage, Yamaha Japan was exporting all bikes from Japan to India.

     Engine: 98.2 cc (5.99 cu in) air-cooled, reed valve two-stroke single
     Fuel capacity: 10.5 L (2.3 imp gal; 2.8 US gal)
     Fuel consumption: 25-40 kmpl
     Seat height: 765 mm (30.1 in)
     Top speed: 110 kmph
     Brakes: Expanding Drum (both front and rear)
     Class: Commuter Sports
